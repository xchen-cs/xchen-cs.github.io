陈翔  is my chinese name, in which the given name 翔(xiang) means "fly freely in the sky, like an eagle".

 

I am now an assistant professor in the Graphics and Parallel Systems Lab, Zhejiang University. I received both my Ph.D and B.Sc. degrees in Computer Science from Zhejiang University.

 

My current research interests mainly include fabrication-aware design, image analysis/editing, shape modeling/retrieval and computer-aided design, but are not limited to these. Fundamentally, I am excited about all the topics with beautiful geometry.
