I am an associate professor in the State Key Lab of CAD&CG, and a Qiushi Young Scholar with Zhejiang University. My research interests include physics-based simulation, shape modeling/retrieval, image analysis/editing, and computer-aided design. I'm looking for self-motivated students, especially those with strong CS/Mathematics/Physics background. Feel free to drop me an email if you are interested.

I received my Ph.D and B.Sc. from Computer Science College and Cho Kochen Honors College of Zhejiang University, respectively. I was a visiting scholar in Computer Graphics group at Columbia University, and a visiting student in Geometric Modelling and Scientific Visualization Center at KAUST and Data Intelligence and Tools group at Microsoft Research Asia.
